Our Values

At Tefli, our values are rooted in care, curiosity, and trust. We create educational content that helps children learn about themselves, the world around them, and the culture they belong to in a simple, playful, and age-appropriate way. From everyday habits like washing hands to important topics such as body awareness, safety, respect, and Moroccan culture, Tefli aims to guide young minds with kindness, clarity, and imagination. Our goal is to make learning feel natural, joyful, and meaningful for every child.

Characters

Every character is designed with care, because every detail matters. From facial expressions and colors to clothing, gestures, and personality, we create characters that feel familiar, friendly, and meaningful to children. Our stories are carefully simplified so young viewers can understand them easily, while still keeping the message clear, useful, and engaging. Inspired by Moroccan culture, we make sure our clips reflect the world children know: its values, traditions, language, warmth, and everyday life. Through thoughtful design and storytelling, Tefli turns important lessons into simple, playful, and culturally rooted moments of learning.

Merch

Our merch extends the identity of Tefli into simple, everyday objects. Each item carries the Tefli logo as a symbol of care, trust, and joyful learning, allowing children and families to feel connected to the brand beyond the screen.
